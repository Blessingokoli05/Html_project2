Brown Panama cuisine

APPERTIZERS

  • Canape : Usually a thin slice of bread that is cut into shapes

  • Chip'n'Dip : A potatoes chips chips, crackers, or raw vegetables that are used to scoop up a dip savory creamy mixture

  • Baked Buffalo Wings : Deep-fried unbreaded chicken wings or drumsticks coated with a vinegar and cayenne-pepper hot sauce mixed with butter

MAIN COURSES

  • Spaghetti Balognese : Spaghetti bolognese consists of long strings of pasta with a meat sauce made with minced beef, bacon and tomatoes.

  • Honey Garlic Fish : It is a flavorful dish made using fish fillets and a delicious combination of honey, soy sauce, and garlic. Serve it with quinoa or rice.

  • Schezwan Fried Rice : cooked rice is tossed with spicy sauces and veggies.

DESSERTS

  • Cheesecake : Its a dessert made with a soft fresh cheese , eggs, and sugar.

  • Ice cream : A frozen dessert typically made from milk or cream that has been flavoured with a sweetener, either sugar or an alternative, and a spice

  • Chicken Pie : Boneless, skinless chicken breasts (cut into cubes) mashed and mixed with flour
